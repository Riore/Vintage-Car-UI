Benefits of Membership

We send out a monthly newsletter with information about upcoming events, goods for sale, and Club developments.Every three months, members get a prize publication that highlights and reports on ouractivities.
Discounts: Members get discounts from a variety of partners, such insurers, booksellers, recovery services, auction houses, and vintage automobile dealers, among others.

Employee Benefits

Valley workers can enroll in our long-term disability, life insurance, and extended health insurance packages after completing their probationary period.

  • Our "in-house" dental and vision reimbursement plan completes our expanded benefits package for our personnel.
  • We pay a percentage of your vision and dental costs for you and your family.
  • When you work at Valley, you can relax knowing that your family is in good hands.
