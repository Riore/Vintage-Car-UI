Show And Event

Vintage Show & Event in 2021

We bring our colleagues together as a team through our monthly staff activities, in addition to creating a balanced work and life environment. We hold a Dart Tournament every year to welcome in the new year, and everyone in the company is asked to join us for a night of darts, supper, and fun. During the summer, we hold a family-friendly BBQ where you may invite the whole family! We always have wonderful entertainment, kids' activities, and, of course, delicious food. Valley also kicks off the summer with a motorbike trip for its employees. Employees are welcome to bring their own bicycles or utilize one of ours to ride across the Lower Mainland and Fraser Valley, pausing for lunch and some beautiful scenery.

Place

Mille Miglia

(Italian: “Thousand Miles”), the most famous of the Italian road races for automobiles. Although the course was changed 13 times in the 23 years the race was run, it often started and ended in Rome, winding through the mountains and smaller towns of Italy.
